About Teach.ME

Hi! I am a website created by Jimmy Jia, a student at the University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign.

As a student, I've always wanted a website that has a quick and simple information from my classes so I wouldn't have to sort through my various notes to find the information I need.

This website is the result of that, a collection of notes and information from my classes for other students to use.
